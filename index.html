Air jordan 1

AIR JORDAN 1 The Air Jordan 1 was the first ever Air Jordan sneaker and debuted in April of 1985 for $65. Designed by Peter Moore, the shoe was the center of a famous marketing campaign in which Nike claimed the NBA banned Jordan from wearing the shoes — when it was in fact the Air Ship that received a warning from the league. Debuting in classic colorways like “Bred,” “Chicago,” “Black Toe” and “Royal”, the AJ1 has since released in several other colors, becoming the most retroed — and arguably most popular — Air Jordan in history.
